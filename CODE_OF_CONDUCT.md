Expected behavior for community members - BE KIND, RESPECT DIFFERING OPINIONS, GIVE FEEDBACK TOWARD PEOPLES IDEAS AND CONTRIBUTIONS, TAKE RESPONSIBILITY FOR FAILURES.

Unacceptable behavior examples - HARRASMENT, INSULTS, PERSONAL ATTACKS. THINGS SUCH AS TROLLING OR COMMENTS THAT ARE TARGETED TO SOMEONE. 

How to report violations - IF YOU EXPERIENCE ANY OF THESE, REPORT IT TO THE OWNER OR TO AN ADMIN. 

Enforcement guidelines - WARNING, TEMP BAN, PERMANENT BAN

Community values and principles - THE PRINCIPLE IS TO HAVE A COMMUNITY EVERYONE CAN BE A PART OF AND FLOURISH IN.
